Coco Diet

Coco loves the scent of coffee and grass [food as well but he always loves food anyway] so walk him to those places that have it If there's no dog friendly cafe or grass around your area, just open a cafe or just move to another place that has it, your place of living is no good if Coco doesnt like it. We are all living in Coco's world.

Consult realiable website/ vet before you give him certain new food that he is not normally eat. Don't give him beef with too much oil, give him goat milk instead of cow milk, etc.

He loves milk and cheese charlie's bear treat. If you can find it, please buy it for him.
